# SmartBudgetAI
SmartBudget AI

SmartBudget AI is a project developed as part of the SAI1 module.
Our goal is to build a business case and a working prototype of an AI tool that helps users gain clarity over their personal finances.

Problem Statement

Many people struggle to understand where their money goes. Bank statements are often messy and do not provide clear insights into spending habits.

Our Solution

SmartBudget AI translates raw financial data into simple, human-readable insights.
Core features include:

Expense Categorization – Automatically groups transactions (housing, food, transport, leisure, subscriptions).

Pattern Detection – Identifies unusual or recurring spending (e.g., multiple subscriptions).

Savings Suggestions – Shows potential savings from small lifestyle adjustments.

Goal Planning (optional) – Track savings goals with progress updates.

Plain-Language Insights – Instead of just charts, the AI explains finances in natural language.

Overspending Alert (new) – Gentle push notifications when spending patterns exceed normal levels.

Value Proposition

Clarity – Understand your finances at a glance.

Savings – Detect hidden costs and spending leaks.

Guidance – Personalized, actionable advice.

Peace of Mind – Stay in control of your budget.

Business Model

We are evaluating different revenue models, including:

Freemium – Free for basic use, premium features available.

B2B2C with Banks – Banks license the app and offer it to customers.

Partnerships with Payment Apps – Integration with apps like Twint, Revolut, or Yuh.

Employee Benefits – Companies offer it as a financial wellbeing tool.

Tech Stack (Prototype)

Standalone Mobile App – User-friendly interface available on iOS and Android.

Secure Bank API Connections / File Uploads – Users can either connect their bank accounts directly or upload transaction files.

OpenAI LLM – Explains financial data in plain language.

Dedicated AI Model per User – Each user’s model adapts to their unique spending habits, enabling more personalized insights and recommendations.
